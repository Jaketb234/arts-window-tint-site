Common questions

What customers usually ask before booking

Still unsure? Text Art and include your vehicle year, make, and model.

How long does a tint job usually take?

Timing depends on the vehicle and amount of film being installed. Most tint installations are completed the same day, while smaller jobs may take less than an hour.

Is ceramic tint worth it?

Ceramic film generally provides stronger heat rejection than basic film while also reducing UV exposure. It is usually the better option for drivers who prioritize comfort and interior protection.

How dark can I legally go in Georgia?

Georgia regulates visible light transmission based on vehicle type and window location. Art can explain the available film choices, but customers remain responsible for confirming legal compliance.

What if I already have old or damaged tint?

Old, purple, peeling, or bubbling tint can be removed and replaced. Removal requires additional labor, so mention the existing film when requesting a quote.

Does the film include a warranty?

Qualifying window films include manufacturer-backed warranty coverage. Exact terms depend on the product selected and will be explained before installation.
