FAQs

How long does a tint job usually take?

It depends on the vehicle and how much film is being installed, but all tint installs are done the same day. Smaller jobs (like just the front two windows) can be done in under an hour.

Is ceramic tint worth it?

Ceramic films help to block out more heat and UV rays compared to standard dyed films. If you care about keeping the interior cooler and protecting leather seats, ceramic is usually the way to go.

How dark can I legally go in Georgia?

Georgia has specific limits for front, rear, and windshield tint. For Coupes and sedans, 32% on the sides and rear is whats legal. For trucks and SUVs, the front two windows must be 32%, and the back half can be as dark as desired. On all windsheilds in Georgia, tinting the entire thing is illegal, but a 6 inch visor strip is allowed. Art can help with any further questions so that you’re not guessing.

What if I already have old or bad tint?

Old, purple, or bubbling tint can be removed and replaced. Tint removal takes extra time, and adds an additional charge, so mention it when you reach out for a quote.
